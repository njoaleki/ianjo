chicken stew

The chicken stew is a delicacy all a cross africa continent but mostly is a kenyan recipe.

chicken was the most ordered delicacy in kenya,atleast 45million kenyans frequently consume chicken with 27.6 percent of the total population partaking the meal once a week.

while chicken was the most popular choice,followed by pizza burgers and alcohol,there was also an increase in the number of kenyans trying indian and eastern european cuisines.

according to the FAO about 50 million birds are slaughtered in kenya annualy with 92.6percent of the population frequently eating chicken.

Ingredients

  • kienyeji chicken
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt to taste
  • C.oil

Procedure

  1. Boil the kitchen with salt;'garlic and ginger till tender.
  2. In a pan heat c.oil till hot.
  3. In a pan heat c.oil till hot.
  4. Add your boiled chicken in batches.Fry till brown.
  5. In a sufuria fry onion till it start browning.
  6. Add in your chicken and stir.
  7. Add blended tomatoes and cover the sufuria.
  8. Cook under low heat till tomatoes are fully cooked.
  9. Add little water.Let it simmer for some minutes.
  10. Garnish with dhania.

PISHORI RICE

pishori rice is low in fat, cholesterol and sodium and hence makes a perfect food for those with high blood pressure and requiring a low-sodium diet.

The calcium in rice helps lower blood pressure.

pishori rice is a good source of complex carbohydrates which gives us the energy we need.

Ingredients
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • onion
  • tomatoes
  • salt to taste
  • C.oil
procedure
  1. using cooking oil
  2. then add onions and cook for about 1 minute until softened
  3. then add garlic and cook for another 30 seconds
  4. add rice and turmeric
  5. stir well to coat
  6. add all remaining Ingredients and mix well

  9. open lid and remove cinnamon stick thyme stems and bay leaf
